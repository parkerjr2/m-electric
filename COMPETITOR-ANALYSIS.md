1. **Out-content the franchises** with 50+ blog posts in 12 months covering aluminum wiring, NEC code updates, panel sizing tools, EV charger brand comparisons. Franchises won't match this with their templated content engines.

2. **Out-personalize** — every franchise call goes to a call center. Lean into "Marshall answers the line personally" in headlines, not just FAQs. Use it as a CTA microcopy throughout.
