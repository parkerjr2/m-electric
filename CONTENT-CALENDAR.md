- Question-shaped H1 + H2 structure
- Founder voice (Marshall named in body where authentic)
- Real photos where possible (from job gallery + new shoots)
- Internal links to relevant service pages
- FAQ schema for ranked-question coverage

## Production workflow

**For each post**:

1. **Outline (Marshall)** — 15 min. Marshall provides 3–5 bullet points of "things real Tulsa customers ask about this" and any project examples / photos.
2. **Draft (writer)** — 2–3 hours. ~1,200 words, founder voice, local specificity.
3. **Review (Marshall)** — 15 min. Validate technical accuracy, add any direct-quote sentences in his voice.
4. **Polish + publish (dev/SEO)** — 30 min. Schema, internal links, OG image, FAQ entries, push live.

**Total**: ~3–4 hours per post. At 4 posts / month, that's 12–16 hours of writing time + 2 hours of Marshall's review time.

**Cost benchmarks**:
- DIY (Marshall writes everything): Marshall's time only. ~6 hours / post = 24 hours/month.
- Outsourced writer ($0.10/word, 1,200w avg): $120/post × 4 = $480/month.
- Hybrid (Marshall outlines + reviews, writer drafts): $480/month + 2 hours of Marshall.

Recommended: **Hybrid** — Marshall's voice/expertise is the differentiator, but he doesn't need to write every word.
